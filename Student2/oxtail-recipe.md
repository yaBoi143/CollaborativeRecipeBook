"# Oxtail Recipe"



\## Ingredients

\- 2kg oxtail

\- Salt and pepper

\- 2 onions (chopped)

\- 3 carrots (sliced)

\- 3 potatoes (cubed)

\- 3 cloves garlic

\- Beef stock

\- Oil for browning



\## Instructions

1\. Season oxtail with salt and pepper.

2\. Brown in a pot with oil.

3\. Add onions and garlic, cook until soft.

4\. Add beef stock and simmer for 2–3 hours.

5\. Add carrots and potatoes halfway through cooking.

6\. Cook until meat is tender and sauce is rich.



\## Description

A slow-cooked, hearty oxtail stew perfect for a warm meal.
